SIRINTIP–TRIBUS

2018 | Role : Art Director, Graphic Designer, Illustrator

 

Artwork design and identity design for Sirintip debut album 'Tribus.'

Sirintip is an award-winning who is stretching across three continents and cultures (Thailand, Sweden, and America) Tribus — produced by three-time Grammy winner Michael League of Snarky Puppy. Her music blurs the boundaries of pop, R&B, electronic and jazz. The album artwork unfolds the idea of Tribus into visual as three layers of Sirintip. "Tribus means “three” in Latin, a number that serves as the overriding theme on the album — three continents, three languages and the three relationships we all share (to the world, to other people and ourselves)."
