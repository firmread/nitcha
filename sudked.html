Sudkedtedsakran

Logo design

Role : Graphic Designer

2012

 

Credits

Graphic design - Nitcha Tothong

Client - RayMac TV

Another collaboration with Raymac team, which I worked with on the project 1081009 muangnarak TV program. This project named “Sudkedtedsakran”, it is a travel show that only air during special holidays. The sense of festive and a journey to the edge are the main inspiration of the logo.  Lettering have connected lines which is also a metaphor for continued journey while diagonal lines are to convey the sense of moving through the places.
