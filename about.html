ABOUT

Nitcha Fame Tothong is a visual artist/ animator/ creative technologist. Her works explore contemporary technology’s impact on daily life. She also has extensive background in music industry and indie music scene in Thailand. From producing album artwork to directing music video.

 

She is passionated maker that loves handcrafting and cross medium pollination. She is fascinated on visual aesthetics, human-computer interaction and poetic technology. She recently graduated from MFA at Parsons School of Design, Design and Technology program in New York.
