ABOUT

I'm a Bangkok-based graphic designer, art director and animator, born and raised in Bangkok, Thailand. I love to work across various medium including animation, graphic design and illustration. I’m passionate with art making that involved handcrafting and working across variety of mediums. Most of my work and heavily inspired by music, I’ve worked on many projects related with music industry and indie music scene in Thailand from album artwork to music video.
