UPRISING–MUSKETEER

2012–Art Direction, Graphic Design

 

Artwork design and identity design for "Uprising," Musketeers' second album released in 2012, reflects the band's musical growth and journey through long and winding roads to overcome obstacles to become a great band. The stone mountain stages the difficulties of barrier while rising to the next step.

 

The graphical elements, such as a stone and compass, are to inspire the mind of the journey and their determination to keep going on this route. Composition structured used also inspired by lines from the band's logo.
