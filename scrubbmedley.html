This visual for live concert of Thai indie pop band “Scrubb” is for a medley of two songs. The lyric of first song is about lovers in relationship in metaphor of two stars orbiting around each other. Visual concept followed this narrative. The second song is about taking time in relationship to get that answer from your heart. Even though the characters had been separated from each others. They try to find the way to meet again because the know that they are the answer for each other.
