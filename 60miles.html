Album Artwork

Role : Graphic Designer

2011

 

 

 

Credits

Art direction - Odd Rojvanich

Graphic design - Nitcha Tothong

Photography - Alongkorn Sriprasert

Client - Believe records

“Color in White”, a second album of Thai indie rock artist 25 hours. The band goes through overhaul graphical branding. The concept of color and white is to reflect the band’s fresh and colorful music. The graphic pattern used inspires from visual of kaleidoscope camera where you can see things in front of you in a new colorful dimension. Which also evoke a sense of fun and colorful. The outer cover is in white, while unfolding the colorful booklet represent that colorful splash of music their music gives.
