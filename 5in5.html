5 in 5

Multiple 5 projects in 5 days

Role :  Designer

2014

 

This 5 projects are parts of Major Studio1 class, MFA D+T. They were done in 5 days straight; 1 project per 1 day. The projects are not connected but there are a theme about my self and my daily habit.

Day 1 : Visual Diary

Sep 01, 2014

 

The first day, I thought about activity that we do everyday. One of that is record what is happen in a day. We usually write it in our diary but I chose to take a video of it. I went to Dia : beacon so I record the way to go there. I believe that take a rest and finding inspiration are important for designers as much as working.

Day 2 : Feeling collector

Sep 02, 2014

 

The second day I decided to make project with the idea I have been wanted to do for long time combines with idea that to do project specificly to the day. I did crowd source animation from people around me. The idea is a simple phase we talk everyday "How are you?" Do we really want to know other people's feeling? Do we really feel what we tell other people? Does the feeling we say really capture that feeling on that moment?
