5 in 5

Multiple 5 projects in 5 days

Role :  Designer

2014

 

This 5 projects are parts of Major Studio1 class, MFA D+T. They were done in 5 days straight; 1 project per 1 day. The projects are not  series. There have themes about my self and my daily habit.

Day 1 : Visual Diary

Sep 01, 2014

 

The first day, I thought about activity that we do everyday. We are barely to remember places we go, stuff we do so I recorded what is happen in a day. We usually write it in our diary but I chose to take a video of it. I went to Dia : beacon so I record the way to go there. I believe that take a rest and finding inspiration are important for designers as much as working.

Day 2 : Feeling collector

Sep 02, 2014

 

The second day I decided to make project with the idea I have been wanted to do for long time, which is crowd source animation. Combining with idea that to do a project specifically to a day. I collected drawing from people around me with a simple que we use everyday, "How are you?"

 

Do we really want to know other people's feeling? Do we really feel what we answer other people? Do we really can capture or describe our feeling on that moment, that day?
