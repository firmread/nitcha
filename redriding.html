Little red riding hood CE Model

Hand painted, Customized CE (Creative Enhancement) Model.

Role : Designer, Illustrator

2010

This hand painted graphic and illustration on a plaster model is inspired from a classic fairy tales, Little Red Riding Hood. The wolf is also designed in the way that incorporated in the this character as an alterego of the protagonist herself, using the feature of the headgear to switch between the two.
