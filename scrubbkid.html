Music video is following name and lyric in the concept of ‘Kids’. The word ‘kids’ had a homophone word in Thai means ‘think’, combine that with the original meaning of children. The music video shot the band in amusement park, the place that represent childhood. While overlayed cel animation is used to represent ‘think’, as children’s imagination.
