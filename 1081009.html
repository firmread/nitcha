1081009 Muangnarak

Soaking digital prints and cel animation

Role : Director, Designer and Animator

2012

 

 

Credits

Lettering design - Nitcha Tothong

Project - Krzysztov, RayMac TV

Client - Tourism Authority of Thailand

Cast - Chintnudda Lamakanond, Phatthi Buntawanit

 

1081009 muangnarak is television program about traveling by RayMac TV,  sponsored by Tourism Authority of Thailand. A series of twelve lettering, each representing cities in Thailand. The design concept took an impression on character of each city like landmark, famous product, or architecture and mix it with mood of that chapter’s journey.
